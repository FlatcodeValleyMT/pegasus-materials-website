A 100% bio-based high-heat polyamide that combines exceptional melt flow with ultra-low moisture uptake — enabling thinner walls, faster charging, and higher manufacturing yields in next-generation USB-C and DDR memory connectors.

Material Overview

The connector material industry has been waiting for.

For consumer electronics, data center servers, and electric vehicles, next-generation connectors demand materials that can accommodate thinner walls, survive reflow soldering without blistering, and enable faster electrical performance. Virella-001 was designed from the ground up to meet and exceed these requirements.

Produced using two bio-based monomers, Virella-001 is a polyamide with a unique molecular architecture that delivers lower water absorption than any incumbent connector polyamide — significantly reducing blistering during reflow soldering — while its high flow properties enable thinner, more precise molding of complex connector geometries.

Key Properties
01
Ultra-Low Moisture Uptake — Dramatically reduces blistering during reflow soldering, cutting scrap rates and improving manufacturing yield.
02
Exceptional Melt Flow — High fluidity enables thinner wall sections and more complex geometries without sacrificing structural integrity.
03
High Heat Resistance — Survives lead-free reflow soldering profiles and extended high-temperature operating environments.
04
Improved Electrical Insulation — Superior dielectric properties enable faster data transfer rates and support tighter connector pitch designs.
05
Color Stability — Maintains aesthetic consistency across thermal cycles — critical for consumer electronics applications.
06
100% Bio-Based — Derived entirely from renewable bio-based monomers, meeting sustainability mandates without sacrificing performance.
