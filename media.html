Pegasus Materials Debuts Bio-based Specialty Materials for Electronics, Aerospace, and 3D Printing

Business Wire November 20, 2025 Read Full Article

Pegasus Materials BV announces the debut of its first two materials — Virella-001 and Virella-002 — alongside an expansion of its Seed funding round. The new investment by venture studio Ferment and existing investor LIOF, alongside prior investment from Fibrant BV, will accelerate commercial scale-up for the electronics, data center, and aerospace industries.

The announcement highlights Virella-001's potential for USB-C interconnects and DDR memory connectors, and Virella-002's evaluation by aerospace and defense partners for structural aircraft components.
