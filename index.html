About
About Pegasus

A new kind of
materials company.

We combine synthetic biology, AI-accelerated chemistry, and deep materials science expertise to engineer polymers that outperform conventional petrochemical solutions — with a smaller environmental footprint.

Founded in 2023 and headquartered at the Brightlands Chemelot Campus in Geleen, Netherlands, Pegasus Materials serves the most demanding customers in the world, and is backed by leading investors and strategics including Fibrant, Ferment, and LIOF.
