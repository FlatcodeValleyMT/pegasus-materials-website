Our Story

Founded in 2023 by materials veterans with over 50 combined years of experience at DSM Engineering Plastics, GE, Apple, and Zymergen — we saw the gap that conventional chemistry couldn't close.

Companies in electronics, aerospace, and advanced manufacturing are pushing beyond what today's petrochemical polymers can deliver. Faster data transfer demands better dielectric properties. Lighter aerospace structures need thermoplastics that withstand extreme heat. 3D-printed parts need to be as strong in the Z-axis as anywhere else.

Pegasus Materials was founded to meet these unmet needs — using bio-based building blocks that give our polymers unique chemical structures unavailable to conventional synthesis, combined with AI-guided molecular design to accelerate discovery from years to months.
